# About Me:

I am a physics PhD student at the University of Connecticut, where I study computational astrophysics under the advisorship of [Dr. Anglés-Alcázar](https://angles-alcazar.physics.uconn.edu/).
I'm currently working on [SWIMBA](@/projects/swimba.md), a reimplementation of the SIMBA galaxy formation in the SWIFT simulation code.
This work, as well previous work I've been involved with, has given me great experience in working efficiently with highly parallel computer systems.
I am also proficient in efficiently handling the output of these simulations, 200Gb of numerical data for each run.
